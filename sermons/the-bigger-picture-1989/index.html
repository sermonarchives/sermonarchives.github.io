The Bigger Picture

This sermon was preached in February of 1989 for Transfiguration Sunday. The text is from Luke 9:34, presumably making this sermon the equivalent of Transfiguration Sunday C.

The Sermon refers to Buena Vista Park in Alma, WI. Here it is on Google Maps.

There is also a reference to the “Jesus of Nazareth” TV mini-series. Here it is on IMDB. The Bigger Picture - Transfiguration Sunday (2/5/1989)
