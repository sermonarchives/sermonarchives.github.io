Easter's Contrasts

  • Sep 22, 2017

Scanned, handwritten sermon for Easter of 1978. My guess is that this was preached at Ascension in Navarino.

Gospel text noted as Matthew 28: 1-10, which likely puts this sermon into Year A for Easter in the Revised Common Lectionary.

ABOUT THE ARCHIVES
Steven M. Larson

This is a site containing the sermon archives of Pastor Steve Larson, who created and preached sermons from 1969 to 1997.

This site was created by his son, Ross.

Sermon Archives uses the Hugo static site templating engine. Material for this site has been obtained from 3.5 inch floppy disks, scanned from paper, and ripped from cassette tapes.

Temporary locations for this data include Trello boards and Google Drive. Links to those temporary locations are available upon request.
